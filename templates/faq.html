Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I start a chat on your website? +

Starting a chat on our website is easy! Simply click on the "Chat" button or the chat icon, and a chat window will open. From there, you can choose a user to chat with and begin your conversation.

2. Can I chat with multiple users simultaneously? +

Yes, our website allows you to chat with multiple users at the same time. You can have different conversations in separate chat windows or tabs, making it convenient to manage your chats.

3. Is my chat history saved on the website? +

Yes, your chat history is saved on our website. You can access your previous chat conversations by clicking on the chat history or conversation log feature. This allows you to review past chats and continue conversations where you left off.

4. How can i reset my Password? +

1. Visit our website's login page.
2. Click on the "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password" link (whichever is available).
3. You will be redirected to a password reset page.
4. Enter the email address associated with your account.
5. Click on the "Reset Password" button.
6. You will receive an email containing a unique link to reset your password.
7. Please check your inbox, including the spam folder, for this email.
8. Click on the link provided in the email.
9. This will take you to a new page where you can enter your new password.
10. Choose a strong and secure password.
11. We recommend using a combination of letters (uppercase and lowercase), numbers, and special characters.
12. Confirm your new password by entering it again.
13. Save the changes, and your password will be successfully reset.
